Classic Tomato Bruschetta

A simple yet elegant appetizer, perfect for any occasion. Fresh, vibrant, and full of flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 French baguette, sliced 1/2-inch thick
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 large tomatoes, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped
  • 1 tbsp balsamic glaze
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your broiler. Arrange baguette slices on a baking sheet and brush with olive oil. Broil for 1-2 minutes per side, until golden brown.
  2. In a medium bowl, combine the diced tomatoes, red onion, minced garlic, and fresh basil.
  3. Drizzle the tomato mixture with balsamic glaze and season with salt and pepper. Stir gently to combine.
  4. Top each toasted baguette slice with a spoonful of the tomato mixture.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy!
